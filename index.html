Success Stories

Emily Chen

Golf taught me that persistance and precision are just as important as power.I'm proud to be part of a growing community of women changing the face of this sport.

Started at age:12

Emily began golfing after watching her father every weekend.Intially one of the only girls at her local club,she now mentors young female golfers and advocates for gender equality in sports.

Jasmine Williams

I never saw many people who looked like me on the golf course growing up.Now I'm showing girls of all backgrounds that they belong in the sport too.

Achievements:U.S. Womens Open participant,Collage All-American

Started at age:15

Jasmine discoverd gof through a community outreach program in her neighborhood. Despite starting later than many of her competitors,her natural talent and dedication helped her earn a collage scholarship and eventually compete professionally.

Margaret Foster

I took up golf at 50 when most people thought i was too old to learn.Now at 68,I've won senior tournaments and shown that it's never too late to pursue your passion.

Achievements:Senior Women's Amateur Champion,Club Champion

Started at age:50

Margaret always wanted to play golf but spent her younger years focused on her career and family.After retirement,she finally took lessons and quickly fell in love with the sport.She now runs a program encouraging older women to try golf.
